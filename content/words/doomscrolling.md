## Definition

Compulsively scrolling through negative or anxiety-inducing news or social media content.

## Origin

The term emerged during the COVID-19 pandemic and became associated with excessive digital media consumption.

## Example

> “I stayed up until 2 AM doomscrolling TikTok and news feeds.”

## Related Terms

- digital fatigue
- algorithmic anxiety
- information overload
