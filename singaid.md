# Singaid
### (*idiom, idiomatic expression*)

## Defintion

Said to someone who has just hiccuped, as a polite remark; 
a remark offering good wishes of hiccups passing.


## Use of the word **singaid**

person one: *has the hiccups*

person two: "singaid"

person one: *hiccup* "Thank you so much" *hiccup* "I hate the—" *hiccup* "hiccups—" *hiccup*

## Etymology and Word History

*Singaid* is derived from:
1. The medical term for hiccups, *singultus*, which is Latin for gasp or sob; and
2. The English word *aid*, meaning to provide support, assistance, or help

Combined, *singaid* acts as a wish of aid when someone has the hiccups.
